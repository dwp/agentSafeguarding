Report an incident

Your protection and safety are our top priority. If you have been involved or witnessed an incident where you have felt threatened, abused or endangered please report it using this form.

The information provided will help us support you in the best way and will be used to learn how to avoid similar situations in the future. If you don't have all the required information at this stage, don't worry, you will be able to add them at a later stage.

If you feel distressed and in need of immediate support please refer to our Support line

To report an incident you need the following information:

  • Your details
  • Details of the citizen
  • Information of when and where the incident happened
  • Contacts of witnesses (if any)
