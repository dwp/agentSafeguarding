Witness report

In case you witnessed an incident it is your duty to provide a statement about the situation in the next 24 hours. If you received an Incident ID regarding the incident please use it to link your report to the correct incident. If you didn't receive an Incident ID please try to identify the citizen who was involved.

To submit a Witness report you need the following information:

  • Incident ID

    OR

  • Citizen details and date of the incident

For specific case you can report an unknown citizen (e.g. a break in during night time)
