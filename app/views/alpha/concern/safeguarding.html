Report an incident or concern

When to use this form:

  • To report an incident with a member of the public
  • To report the behaviour of a member of the public which may lead to a risk for you or other staff members
  • To report an information which may help prevent a risk during the interaction with a member of the public
  • To report a concern about the health or wellbeing of a member of the public

If you feel distressed and in need of immediate support please refer to our Help Employee Assistance

To report an incident or concern you need the following information:

  • Your details
  • Details of the citizen you want to report about
  • Information of when and where the incident or concern happened
  • Contacts of witnesses (if any)

If necessary you can report an incident when the identity of the citizen is unknown (e.g. somebody broke inside the office during night time)
