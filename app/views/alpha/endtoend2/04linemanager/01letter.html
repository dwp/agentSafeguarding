Request injuction (England and Wales)
Build case Build case

A judicial order restraining a person from beginning or continuing an action threatening or invading the legal right of another, or compelling a person to carry out a certain act, e.g. to make restitution to an injured party.

Request interdiction (Scotland)
Build case Build case

An interdict is a civil court order that tells a person not to do something or to stay away from you, your children or a specific place, such as your house. In Scotland if a person doesn't stick to an interdict, the police might be able to arrest them if the interdict gives them the power to do so.
