Incident Breakdown

Written by: Whitney Earl

What started the situation?
I was explaining to Robert why he was not eligible anymore for UC. His civil partner earned £6000 last month therefore he lost the right for UC. He thought that was unfair and no matter how calmly I was explaining the problem, he just kept interrupting me and shouting.
What happened during the interaction?
Robert shouted at me for the whole time: “You useless cu*%, you don’t give a fu%@ about me! You can’t do this to me! You are just a keyboard warrior. It is easy for you to take this decision behing your comfy desk.” It was extremely offensive and I tried to explain that was not my fault, it’s just how things work but he didn’t listen.
How did you handle the interaction?
I tried to calm him down, but he shouted very loud and started getting closer to me, pointing the finger and leaning on the table. Every time I opne my mouth he just shouted over me.
How did the interaction end?
Sartaq came from his desk and asked him politely to lower his voice. He didn’t worked. Finally Mike the G4S came and together him and Sartaq managed to escort Robert outside.
How did you feel about the interaction?
I felt threatened and miserable. It was horrible to be shouted at in that way. I felt vulnerable as he was leaning towards me and I thought that he could have grabbed me or punched me at any time. I was just too scared to do anything.

Line manager recommendations

Written by: Sadiq Assam

In your opinion what could have been done to prevent the incident to start?
Robert already received a written warning after leaving some nasty comments on the journal. We didn’t expected this explosion of anger however. Maybe we could have called him on the phone when he booked the appointment with us.
In your opinion what could have been done to keep the member of staff safer during the interaction?
The citizen came very close to grab the staff member. Would have been better to have a screen between them to prevent that possibility.
Did the staff handle the situation at the best of their abilities?
I think so, given the circumstances.
In your opinion was the end of the interaction handled in the best way?
At the time it seems that only the intervention of the security staff managed to stop the behaviour of the citizen. Maybe we should have give Robert a more formal Oral warning in the end.
Did you provide immediate support to the member of staff?
Yes. I invited Whitney to take some time off and we checked with her straigth away to make sure she was fine. It would be good to keep giving her support and to make sure that we add a screen as soon as possible.

An interdict is a civil court order that tells a person not to do something or to stay away from you, your children or a specific place, such as your house.
