Help with date and time
You may be unable to estimate the date and time of the incident. If the incident began during an appointment or a phone call, you can use the start time of the appointment or the call instead.

If the incident involved time-stamped written communication (for example, an email, a tweet or a message in the customer's Journal), enter the date and time that the customer sent the message.

If the incident involved written communication with no time stamp (for example, a letter or graffiti), enter the date and time that you read the message.
