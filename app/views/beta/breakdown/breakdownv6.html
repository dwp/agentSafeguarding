1. Why was the customer in contact with us?
For example: I called John for a routine welfare check.

2. How did the incident start? Was there a specific trigger?
Please report the exact words that everyone used.
For example: I asked John to come into the office next week, and he raised his voice and shouted “I’m not coming to the bloody job centre.”

3. What were the issues with the customer’s behaviour?
Tell us what they said and did, and report the exact words that everyone used.
For example: John refused to answer my questions and said “you’re a stupid cow.”

4. Tell us what happened next. How did you try to resolve the situation, and how did the customer react?
Please report the exact words that everyone used.
For example: I said, “Stop swearing at me. I am trying to help you.” But the customer continued to shout and said, "You're not bloody helping me."
