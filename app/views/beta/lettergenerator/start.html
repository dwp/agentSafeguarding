Letter generator (proof of concept)

Design notes

Hypothesis - Format and content

We believe that:
simplified warning letter format and content will be sufficient to help users understand why they are receiving a letter, the immediate consequences for them and the potential future consequences

To verify that we will:
undertake usability testing with approximately 5 non-expert citizens

And measure:
their understanding of the letter, specifically:

  • why have you receiving this letter?
  • how does this letter affect the way you will deal with DWP?
  • what will happen if you are involved in another incident?

We are right if:
testing participants are able to correctly identify the cause of, result of and future consequences of receiving a warning letter.

Hypothesis - Access to benefits

We believe that:
there may be a requirement to include content about continued access to benefits in warning letters (but equally users might not need this content as it might be clear by omission)

To verify that we will:
take letters into usability testing without this content

And measure:
users understanding of the impacts of the letter, specifically:

  • do you think this letter will have an impact on any benefits you receive from DWP?

We are right if:
testing participants identify that existing content is needed to clarify access to benefits
