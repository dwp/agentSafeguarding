Email 1: Line manager instruction email

Email sent to line manager when one of their team reports an incident.

(Subject line) URGENT: Someone in your team was involved in an incident ((incidentReferenceNumber))

Dear ((First name)) ((Surname)),

Whitney Earl reported an incident at 14.03 on 31 October 2022 (((incidentReferenceNumber))).

As their line manager, you have a duty of care to provide them with support, and to help them access any additional support they need.

For security reasons, we cannot provide any information about the incident in this email.


What to do now

Speak to Whitney as soon as possible to determine what support they need. This might include time away from their job.

Advise them that staff can access free counselling and support through PAM Assist.

What to do regularly

Make sure that:

  • everyone in your team knows how to report incidents and how to check the Staff Protection List
  • your team are always up to date on their procedural and Keeping Safe training
  • you regularly review and circulate the Incident Management Plan, and carry out drills
