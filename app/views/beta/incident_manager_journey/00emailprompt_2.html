Email 2: To the LINE MANAGER (when they are not the incident manager)



(Subject line) URGENT: Review incident report INC-123-456-789

Dear [Name Surname],

Someone in your team reported this incident at 14.03 on 7 June 2022.

As their line manager, you have a duty of care to provide them with support, and to help them access any additional support they need.

For security reasons, we cannot provide any information about the incident in this email.



What happens next

We’ve sent a notification to the incident manager who is best placed to review the incident report. Once they’ve reviewed it, we will forward it to a case reviewer and then to a decision maker.

Your team member will receive an email within 48 hours to tell them the decision about this customer.



Thank you,
Agent Safeguarding.
