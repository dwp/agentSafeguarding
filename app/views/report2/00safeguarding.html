Report an incident or concern

When to use this form:

  • If you have been directly involved in an incident it is your right and duty to report it as soon as possible
  • If you were not directly involved but you witnessed an incident you should add your witness report to the incident: please check the Incident reports list
  • If you noticed a behaviour of a citizen than could potentially escalate into an incident effecting either the citizen himself or your colleagues

If you feel distressed and in need of immediate support please refer to our Help Employee Assistance

To report an incident or concern you need the following information:

  • First name, last name and date of birth of the citizen you want to report
  • Information of when and where the incident or concern happened
