What started the situation?

What happened during the interaction?
Customer obviously upset but wasn't listening to the explanation. He became angry and started to be abusive, stating: "You don't care about me! You are just a bunch of bastards!" You talk just because you are safe behind your desks. It then became physical as he upturned the desk knocking the keyboard, laptop and mouse which did hit me
How did you handle the interaction?
Previous attempts to give full explanation didn't calm customer down and it escalated into the upturning of the desk.
How did the interaction end?
The intervention of G4S who escorted the customer away and into a safer place. Because it became physical and he'd damaged property, the police were called and on their arrival he was taken into their custody. Crime report number: 1234.
How did you feel about the interaction?
Initially I was calm and understood it to be a general appointment. I thought once the customer was made aware of the claim regulations he would agree that the UC claim was now inappropriate. Unfortunately he didn't and became angry. I became a little nervous when he raised his voice and began swearing but was not at all prepared for the physical actions. At this point I became increasingly concerned but was relieved to see G4s support on hand. Now the incident is over. I'm a little nervous and shaking, relieving the incident to see if my behaviour increased his anger, was there anything I could have done differently.
